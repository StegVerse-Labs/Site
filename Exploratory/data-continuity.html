Data Continuity

Reconstructable state relationship across transition.

Research-stage notice: This page is exploratory, subject to revision, and not a production claim.

Core Thesis

Data Continuity is not storage. It is reconstructable state relationship across transition.

Continuity requires a recoverable relationship between prior state, transition, post-state, residue, record, and reconstruction path.

Continuity Chain

S0 --u1/r1--> S1 --u2/r2--> S2 --u3/r3--> S3

Failure Modes

Broken chain, ambiguous state, authority loss, context loss, hash drift, replay failure, silent mutation, and horizon loss.
