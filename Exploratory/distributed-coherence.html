Distributed Coherence

Coherence among multiple bounded systems that are each changing.

Research-stage notice: This page is exploratory, subject to revision, and not a production claim.

Core Thesis

Distributed Coherence is the preservation of coherent relation among systems that are each changing.

Local validity is not sufficient. A node may pass locally while the distributed ecosystem fails globally.

Distributed Condition

C_i(t) ≥ C_min for all affected i
C_global(t) ≥ C_global_min
R_global(t) ≥ R_global_min
DaCo_integrity == true

Failure Modes

Local pass/global fail, split-brain coherence, authority drift, lag-induced invalidity, receipt divergence, quarantine divergence, and reconciliation failure.
