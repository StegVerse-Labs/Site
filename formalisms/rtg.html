What RTG currently means

RTG names the possible relative geometry underneath StegVerse transition governance. The Transition Table is the public representation layer. RTG is the candidate structure that may explain how transition positions, observation windows, admissibility pressure, coupling, confidence, darkness, and emission readiness relate.

RTG should be treated as a structured pre-formalism: stronger than an idea, weaker than completed proof.
