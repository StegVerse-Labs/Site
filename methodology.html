Methodology

StegVerse evaluates production reality, not intent.

Principles

  • Evidence over self-attestation
  • Cross-domain synthesis (AI + governance + trust systems)
  • Focus on interaction failures where disciplines overlap
  • Board-safe clarity without overpromising

What we examine

  • AI/automation pathways and escalation points
  • Governance claims vs operational practice
  • Identity and credential lifecycles (humans + service accounts)
  • Trust boundary drift and privilege creep
  • Audit trail continuity through failure and recovery

What we deliver

  • Defensible findings in executive language
  • A prioritized risk register
  • A remediation roadmap that your team can execute without dependency
