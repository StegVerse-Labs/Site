Math-Solver
Governance Adapter.

This page publishes the public posture for the math-solver bridge: a governed packet structure for asking a solver or model to perform formalism work without granting it free-form authority.

Papers & formalism sources RTG STCM Governance tests
Boundary: this page is a public mirror for the adapter concept. It does not claim solver correctness, mathematical proof, or production authority.

Adapter purpose

The math-solver adapter governs formalism-development actions. It asks what a solver is allowed to attempt, what artifact it must return, what claim may be made from that artifact, and what receipt or replay posture is required before the next instruction.

Governed action chain

formalism state

→ solver or model task
→ returned artifact
→ claim/cost confirmation
→ receipt or replay posture
→ next admissible instruction
