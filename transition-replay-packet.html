Replay Scope

T13 Receipt-Bound Transition

Replays whether pre-state, action, and post-state are bound into deterministic receipt material.

T14 Reconstruction Transition

Replays whether reconstructed end-state hash matches observed end-state hash.

Replay Steps

  1. Open data/transition-replay-packet-v1.json to confirm the replay packet declaration.
  2. Open data/transition-replay-fixtures-v1.json.
  3. For T13, recompute hashes for pre_state, action, and post_state.
  4. Build receipt material from those hashes and recompute receipt_material_hash.
  5. For T14, recompute the observed and reconstructed end-state hashes.
  6. Confirm observed and reconstructed end-state hashes match.
  7. Compare the computed values against data/transition-replay-verifier-v1.json.
  8. Run tools/transition_replay_check.py through the Transition Replay Check workflow.

What This Replay Verifies

  • T13 replay verifies receipt binding from canonical JSON state/action material.
  • T14 replay verifies reconstruction equality from canonical JSON end-state material.
  • The current verifier verdict is ALLOW for the selected T13/T14 fixture set.
  • MS-011 remains the released public verification basis until MS-012 is promoted.

What This Does Not Claim

  • It does not claim all transition elements are independently replayed.
  • It does not claim final publication readiness.
  • It does not claim unbounded autonomous replay.
  • It documents a narrow replay packet for the first receipt-backed evidence layer.
