Where Governed
Admissibility Applies.

This page explains the range of disciplines where governed admissibility directly applies and why testers in those disciplines should test outputs before they become claims, actions, records, or decisions.

Boundary: this map identifies where admissibility testing applies. It does not replace domain expertise, formal proof, professional review, medical review, legal review, financial review, engineering sign-off, or civic authority.

Core question

What is this output, artifact, instruction, transition, or claim allowed to become?
