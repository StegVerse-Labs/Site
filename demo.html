What is happening?

1. Commit Boundary
The backend reconstructs the boundary from commit_states at evaluation time.

2. GOD Divergence
Geometrically Ontological Divergence measures how far the proposed state sits from the boundary. GOD = 1.0 is the surface.

3. Verdict
ALLOW means inside the boundary. DENY means outside but recoverable. FAIL-CLOSED means catastrophic divergence or malformed input.

4. Receipt
The backend returns a SHA-256 receipt hash over the canonical decision record.
