StegVerse Exchange Check

This page is a public-facing placeholder for checking whether an exchange, packet, receipt, or transition artifact is declared, bounded, and reviewable before it is treated as a governance signal.

Boundary

This page does not authorize transitions, declare consent, validate identity, or replace commit-time admissibility checks. It is a review surface only.

Minimum fields

  • Artifact identifier
  • Artifact hash
  • Declared source
  • Declared scope
  • Policy reference
  • Authority class
  • Receipt or evidence pointer
  • Commit-time posture

Result meanings

Reviewable means the artifact exposes enough declared structure to be inspected. It does not mean the transition is admissible.

Incomplete means one or more minimum fields are absent or unclear.

Out of scope means the artifact attempts to claim authority outside the declared review boundary.
