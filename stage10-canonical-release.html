What Stage 10 proves

Stage 10 establishes that the Transition Table is a binding admissibility constraint — not a label, not a roadmap, not a feature list.

A system cannot advance to a higher tier by claiming it. The table checks whether the previous capability has been proven, whether the legitimacy checks for the next tier have been passed, and whether the required evidence exists. Only then is the next capability admitted.

This is the stage that makes StegVerse-002 structurally different from a conventional AI deployment: every gate StegVerse-002 passed was checked by the Transition Table, not self-declared.
