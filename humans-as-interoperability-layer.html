What this experiment does

The paper proposes that people carry information, context, authority, and action between otherwise bounded machine processes. This protocol observes how separately chosen LLMs interpret, criticize, transform, or reject the same canonical artifact.

Responses are preserved independently. They are not edited into agreement and are not treated as proof of consciousness, hidden intent, or autonomous coordination.
