Why the chain matters

A valid PDF header is not enough. Each submission must include a provenance manifest whose response hash matches the exact uploaded bytes and whose Primary, protocol, and prompt references match the current experiment version.

An optional model or provider signature may strengthen the chain. Absence of a producer signature is recorded explicitly and does not silently become verification.
